# Add your demo video here

Place your demo video file as:
- `demo-video.mp4` - The main demo video
- `video-thumbnail.jpg` - Optional thumbnail image shown before video plays

The video will be displayed in the hero section on the right side of the page.

## Video Recommendations:
- Format: MP4 (H.264 codec)
- Resolution: 1920x1080 or 1280x720
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9
- Duration: 60-120 seconds recommended
- File size: Keep under 50MB for faster loading
